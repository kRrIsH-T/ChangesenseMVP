MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT (VERSION 3 - PREMIUM TIER)

This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 1, 2026 ("Effective Date"), by and between CloudTech Innovations, Inc. ("Service Provider") and Enterprise Systems Corp. ("Client").

1. SERVICES
Service Provider shall provide comprehensive enterprise SaaS services including cloud hosting, data storage, advanced analytics, reporting, API access, integrations, dedicated infrastructure, white-label options, and premium support (collectively, "Services"). Detailed service specifications are attached as Exhibits A and B.

2. INITIAL TERM AND RENEWAL PROVISIONS
The Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date for an initial term of three (3) years. Thereafter, it shall automatically renew for successive two-year renewal terms unless either party provides ninety (90) days' prior written notice of non-renewal. Renewal terms shall include annual fee adjustments not to exceed 5%.

3. FEES, PAYMENT, AND BILLING
3.1 Base Monthly Fee. $10,000 per month for base enterprise services.

3.2 Annual Payment Option. Client may prepay annually at $110,000 (2% discount).

3.3 Usage-Based Fees. 
    - Data Storage beyond 10TB: $400 per TB per month;
    - API Calls beyond 5M per month: $0.008 per call;
    - Premium Support Team: additional $2,000 per month.

3.4 Invoicing and Payment Terms. Invoices issued on the 1st of each month; payment due within 15 days of invoice. Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month.

3.5 Price Adjustments. Annual adjustments up to 5% upon ninety (90) days' notice. Service Provider shall credit any downtime-related credits within the following billing cycle.

4. SERVICE LEVELS, UPTIME, AND CREDITS
Service Provider commits to 99.95% system uptime annually (approximately 22 minutes maximum downtime), measured per calendar month on an hourly basis. Scheduled maintenance excluded. Service credits:
- 99.50% to 99.94% uptime: 10% monthly fee credit
- 99.00% to 99.49% uptime: 30% monthly fee credit
- 98.00% to 98.99% uptime: 50% monthly fee credit
- Below 98.00% uptime: 100% monthly fee credit + client right to terminate with 15 days' notice

Credits are the exclusive remedy for uptime shortfalls.

5. SUPPORT SERVICES AND OPERATIONS
Service Provider provides:
    - Premium 24/7/365 support with dedicated account manager;
    - Technical phone support with 1-hour response time for critical issues;
    - Quarterly business reviews and optimization recommendations;
    - Regular security audits and compliance reports;
    - Scheduled maintenance on Sundays 2:00-3:00 AM EST (max 1 per month);
    - Change management and testing environment.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY, SECURITY, AND COMPLIANCE
Each party shall maintain confidentiality. Service Provider shall:
    - Implement industry-leading security (AES-256 encryption, TLS 1.2+);
    - Maintain SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certifications;
    - Perform annual third-party security audits;
    - Provide annual compliance certificates and audit reports;
    - Implement Data Loss Prevention (DLP) controls;
    - Provide data residency options (US, EU, APAC).

7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY AND LICENSING
Service Provider retains all intellectual property rights in Services and platform technology. Client retains all intellectual property in Client Data. Client grants Service Provider limited license to use aggregated, anonymized analytics for service improvement and benchmarking.

8. DATA HANDLING, EXPORT, AND TERMINATION
For Cause Termination: Service Provider may terminate immediately upon material breach by Client uncured for 30 days.
For Convenience: Either party may terminate with 90 days' written notice.
Early Termination by Client: May incur early termination fee of remaining term value up to 6 months.
Data Export: Upon termination, Client Data available for export in standard formats at no additional charge. Data retained for 180 days; permanent deletion thereafter.

9. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION
This Agreement governed by Delaware law. Disputes subject to binding arbitration in New York, New York under JAMS rules.
