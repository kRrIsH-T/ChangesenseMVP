COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT (VERSION 2 - NEGOTIATED)

This Commercial Lease Agreement ("Lease") is entered into as of May 1, 2026 ("Effective Date"), by and between Harbor Point Properties, LLC ("Landlord") and Blue Fern Studio, Inc. ("Tenant").

1. Premises
Landlord leases to Tenant approximately 4,000 rentable square feet located at Suite 210, 18 Market Street, Boston, Massachusetts (the "Premises").

2. Lease Term
The lease term begins on June 1, 2026 and ends on May 31, 2031 (the "Term").

3. Base Rent
Tenant shall pay base rent as follows:
Year 1: $10,900 per month
Year 2: $11,445 per month
Year 3: $12,017 per month
Year 4: $12,618 per month
Year 5: $13,249 per month
Rent is due on the first day of each month.

4. Rent Abatement
Provided Tenant is not in default, base rent for the first two (2) full calendar months of the Term is abated.

5. Security Deposit
Tenant shall deposit $16,000 as a security deposit.

6. Permitted Use
Tenant shall use the Premises for general office, design, software development, customer demonstrations, and related lawful purposes.

7. Operating Expenses; CAM
Tenant shall pay Tenant's proportionate share (8.2%) of common area maintenance, taxes, and insurance (collectively, "Operating Expenses"), estimated at $3.85 per rentable square foot per year for Year 1, subject to annual reconciliation.
Operating Expenses shall exclude capital improvements except those that reduce operating costs or are required by changes in law, amortized over useful life.

8. Utilities
Tenant shall pay all utilities serving the Premises, excluding base building HVAC maintenance, which is included in Operating Expenses.

9. Maintenance and Repairs
Landlord shall maintain structural portions of the building, building systems, and common areas. Tenant shall maintain the interior of the Premises, excluding ordinary wear and tear.

10. Tenant Improvements
Landlord shall provide a tenant improvement allowance of $25.00 per rentable square foot, payable against documented construction costs.

11. Insurance
Tenant shall maintain commercial general liability insurance with limits of not less than $2,000,000 per occurrence and $4,000,000 aggregate, and property insurance for Tenant's personal property.

12. Mutual Indemnity
Each party shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the other from claims arising from the indemnifying party's negligence or willful misconduct.

13. Assignment and Subletting
Tenant shall not assign this Lease or sublet any portion of the Premises without Landlord's prior written consent, which shall not be unreasonably withheld, conditioned, or delayed.

14. Default and Cure
If Tenant fails to pay rent within 10 days after written notice, or fails to perform any non-monetary obligation within 30 days after written notice (or such longer period as reasonably required if diligently pursued), Landlord may exercise available remedies.

15. Late Charge and Interest
Late payments are subject to a late charge of 3% of the overdue amount and interest at the lesser of 10% per annum or the maximum lawful rate.

16. Renewal Option
Tenant may renew for one additional term of five (5) years by delivering written notice not less than 12 months before expiration, at 95% of fair market rent.

17. Early Termination Right
Tenant may terminate this Lease effective after month 42 by paying a termination fee equal to four (4) months of then-current base rent and reimbursing unamortized tenant improvement costs.

18. Governing Law
This Lease shall be governed by the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts.

19. Entire Agreement
This Lease contains the entire agreement between the parties regarding the Premises.

Executed by:
Landlord: Harbor Point Properties, LLC
Tenant: Blue Fern Studio, Inc.
