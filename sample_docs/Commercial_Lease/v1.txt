COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT (VERSION 1 - LANDLORD FORM)

This Commercial Lease Agreement ("Lease") is entered into as of May 1, 2026 ("Effective Date"), by and between Harbor Point Properties, LLC ("Landlord") and Blue Fern Studio, Inc. ("Tenant").

1. Premises
Landlord leases to Tenant approximately 4,000 rentable square feet located at Suite 210, 18 Market Street, Boston, Massachusetts (the "Premises").

2. Lease Term
The lease term begins on June 1, 2026 and ends on May 31, 2029 (the "Term").

3. Base Rent
Tenant shall pay base rent as follows:
Year 1: $11,500 per month
Year 2: $12,075 per month
Year 3: $12,679 per month
Rent is due on the first day of each month.

4. Security Deposit
Tenant shall deposit $23,000 as a security deposit.

5. Use
Tenant shall use the Premises for general office, design, and related administrative use only, and for no other purpose without Landlord's prior written consent.

6. Operating Expenses; CAM
Tenant shall pay Tenant's proportionate share (8.2%) of common area maintenance, taxes, and insurance (collectively, "Operating Expenses"), estimated at $4.20 per rentable square foot per year for Year 1, subject to annual reconciliation.

7. Utilities
Tenant shall pay all utilities serving the Premises, including electricity, internet, and janitorial services.

8. Maintenance and Repairs
Landlord shall maintain the structural portions of the building, including roof and foundation. Tenant shall maintain the Premises, including interior non-structural components.

9. Alterations
Tenant shall not make alterations without Landlord's prior written consent. Landlord may require removal of alterations at lease expiration.

10. Insurance
Tenant shall maintain commercial general liability insurance with limits of not less than $1,000,000 per occurrence and $2,000,000 aggregate, and property insurance for Tenant's personal property.

11. Indemnity
Tenant shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Landlord from claims arising from Tenant's use of the Premises, except to the extent caused by Landlord's gross negligence or willful misconduct.

12. Assignment and Subletting
Tenant shall not assign this Lease or sublet any portion of the Premises without Landlord's prior written consent, which may be withheld in Landlord's reasonable discretion.

13. Default and Remedies
If Tenant fails to pay rent within 5 days after written notice, or fails to perform any non-monetary obligation within 20 days after written notice, Landlord may terminate this Lease and pursue available remedies.

14. Late Charge and Interest
Late payments are subject to a late charge of 5% of the overdue amount and interest at 12% per annum.

15. Renewal Option
Tenant may renew for one additional term of three (3) years by delivering written notice not less than 9 months before expiration, at then-current fair market rent as determined by Landlord.

16. Governing Law
This Lease shall be governed by the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts.

17. Entire Agreement
This Lease contains the entire agreement between the parties regarding the Premises.

Executed by:
Landlord: Harbor Point Properties, LLC
Tenant: Blue Fern Studio, Inc.
